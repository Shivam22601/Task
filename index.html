About IDR

The Institute for Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for careers in digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through collaboration with leading UK universities and industry partners, IDR combines academic insight with practical project experience to equip practitioners with the skills needed to manage modern digital risk challenges.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to develop practical skills in cyber risk and digital resilience.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing advanced risk models, governance frameworks and intellectual property in areas such as AI governance.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement security and risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals and practitioners seeking to develop advanced skills in cyber risk and digital governance.

Our programs support professionals across sectors such as financial services, technology companies and critical infrastructure.
